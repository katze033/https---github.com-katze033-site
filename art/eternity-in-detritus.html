Eternity in Detritus

A selection of digitally manipulated collages and drawings. Each piece begins with the creation of modular and heavily textured paper collages. These modular pieces are then rearranged to build more complex compositions. After scanning, I respond and embellish colors and forms with digital drawing tools. Final works are printed on archival paper and canvas.
