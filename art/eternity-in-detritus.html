Eternity in Detritus

A selection of digitally manipulated collages and drawings. Each piece begins with the creation of modular and heavily textured paper collages. These modular pieces are then combined with one another to form new and surprising pieces. After scanning, I use digital drawing tools to finalize composition and color. Final works are printed on archival paper and canvas.
