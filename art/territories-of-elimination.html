This body of work was developed between December 2019 and November 2020. It comprises compositions generated entirely from javascript using p5js, a creative coding framework.

Automated Conjuration is the manifestation of extraterrestrial worlds through technological collaboration and geometric simplicity. Though the programs I wrote, by nature, adhere to specific pre-determined parameters, the final works are composed by autonomous systems that result in unexpected outputs.
