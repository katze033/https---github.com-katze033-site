About

Josh Katzenmeyer is a multidisciplinary artist and musician harnessing technology, code, collage, and sound to design otherworldly multimedia experiences.

Through guided generative processes, he brings rigid geometric forms into conversation with painterly digital manipulations. The result is an exploration of free will within abstracted realms torn between order and chaos.

He has exhibited in North America, Europe, Asia, and Canada alongside digital displays in the global VR communities connecting them all.
