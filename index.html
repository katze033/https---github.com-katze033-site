Josh Katzenmeyer (@luxpris) is a multidisciplinary artist and musician harnessing technology, code, and collage to design extraterrestrial multimedia experiences.

Through guided generative processes, he brings rigid geometric forms into conversation with painterly digital manipulations. The result is an exploration of free will within abstract worlds torn between order and chaos.

He has exhibited in North America, Europe, Asia, and Canada alongside digital displays in the global VR realms connecting them all.
